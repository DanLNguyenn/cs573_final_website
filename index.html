Project Result

The project was overall a success. By visualizing the full VisPubs.com dataset on Latent Scope, users can easily explore and identify general topics across the data visualization research field. Although there are limitations in the auto-labeling process due to the lack of a paid model API key, this issue is easily addressable in future improvements. Despite some minor noise in the clustering process, the final visualization effectively presents the data in an accessible and meaningful way.
